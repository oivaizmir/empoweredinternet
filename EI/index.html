WHAT IS OUR BIG IDEA

We believe in the Internet as a level playing field. Users should have the same opportunities, the same ability to follow their ambitions and communicate disregarding if they are in New York City, Tokyo, Sydney, as well as Rwamagana, Nyanza and the villages in between rural Rwanda for example. Currently 68% of the planet lives without an internet connection. The economical, educational and democratic possibilities that communities are missing because of this problem are incommensurable. In many locations the dilemma is not lacking internet coverage, instead, it is gaining access to the internet from within a coverage zone.

We propose a social enterprise which mission is focused on taking the traditional Internet Café model and strip it to its minimum expression: a Mobile Internet Café based out of tablets and smartphones that can be used as a tool to fight poverty. A community system organized and driven by members of rural and semi-urban communities. Building upon the empowerment premises, members will enjoy revenue stream generation possibilities while providing services to their communities in areas like education, health and citizenship. Services that could potentially change the course of their lives.

UNLOCKING RURAL HORIZONS

Real change comes when you have reached everyone, everywhere.



COMMUNITY DRIVEN + PROFITABLE


In Rwanda, the average monthly income is approximately $53 USD, less than what a western teenager earns in a day at a fast food restaurant. The income generating potential of a developing country is limited, with it being its own ultra low wage ecosystem completely separated from western market norms. What is unfair is that a skilled worker makes less in the developing world than a worker with the same skill in the west.

The Internet changes this.

With the ability to take intensive online skill building education services, a developing world user who has a few abilities, such as a high level of English, but more importantly a high level of dedication, can build a skill set that makes them internationally competitive in a number of skills. With internet these skills can escape the limited local market to engage in worldwide markets. Now, what if spreading the Internet becomes an income generating activity in itself? Bang!

MOBILE MEANS EVERYWHERE. PERIOD
